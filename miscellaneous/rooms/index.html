THE ROOM
Study

A quiet space in the apartment, perfect for reading, working, or studying, designed to be a place where one can focus and concentrate. It often includes a desk, a chair, and shelves for books. Good lighting is essential, making sure the room feels comfortable and practical for work. The selection of furniture and materials plays a key role in creating a productive environment. A sturdy desk and chair are essential, paired with adequate task lighting, such as a desk lamp, and a storage space, like a bookshelf. The study’s design is carefully curated to optimize both functionality and aesthetic appeal. From an architectural perspective, natural light is maximized, often using large windows or skylights, while artificial lighting is layered to create different moods: task lighting for work, ambient lighting for relaxation.
