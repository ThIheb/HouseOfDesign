House of design

The Project

House of Design is a project from the Information Modelling and Web Technologies course. It presents 19 curated interior design pieces in a custom-designed house, highlighting their artistic and practical value.

By showcasing these objects in real-world settings, the project celebrates interior design as both functional and a significant art form.
