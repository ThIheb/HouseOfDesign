The Spaces

Drawing from iconic pieces of the Milan Triennale, this exhibition celebrates Italian design in four carefully curated spaces: the study, the bathroom, the living room, and the kitchen.

Each space of our exhibit tells a unique story about how we live, offering a window into the profound impact of design on daily living.
